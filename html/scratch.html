Why I Run

Running has helped me develop discipline, consistency, and goal-setting skills.

Training for a half marathon taught me the importance of patience and commitment. Success in distance running comes from consistently showing up and putting in the work over many months.

Training Components

  1. Weekly Training
    • Easy Runs
    • Tempo Runs
    • Long Runs
  2. Recovery
  3. Nutrition

A successful training plan balances hard workouts with recovery. Easy runs build aerobic fitness, tempo runs improve endurance, and long runs prepare runners for race day.

Race Day Strategy

My preferred race strategy is to start conservatively, settle into goal pace, and gradually increase effort throughout the race. Proper pacing is one of the most important factors in achieving a personal best.
